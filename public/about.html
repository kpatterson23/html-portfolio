Who is Kyle Patterson?

I'm a 30 year old, web developer from Philadelphia with a deep love for beautiful websites. I began my career by obtaining an Associate's degree from Strayer University in Information Systems, and later obtaining a Bachelor's degree in Software Development from the same University. I've also attended NuCamp's Front-End Web Development bootcamp to gain expertise in the web development field.

During the summer between my junior and senior years of high school, I went blind from complications with my diabetes. I couldn't afford healthcare at the time and went months without my insulin and lost my eyesight as a result. About a year and two surgeries later, I was able to see again. I'll never forget the sunrise after I unwrapped my eyes and saw the world again with a new perspective. Even though my background is in software development, I know the important of usability for those with visual impairment. While I was in school, my teachers struggled to get resources that could help continue my education in a way I could understand and struggled everyday.

I believe that the goal of every website should be to visually stunning and easily used and navigated by all. We as society tend to forget our most vulnerable, so building a website that can be used by everyone is herculean task that I would like to tackle. If you are like me and believe as I do, take a look at my work and reach out. Together we could build something beautiful!
